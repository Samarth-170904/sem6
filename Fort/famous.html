Famous Forts Around the World

Here are some of the most iconic forts that have left an indelible mark on history:

  • Red Fort (India) - A UNESCO World Heritage Site in Delhi, known for its stunning red sandstone architecture and historical significance.
  • Edinburgh Castle (Scotland) - Perched atop a volcanic rock, this castle offers breathtaking views and a rich history dating back to the 12th century.
  • Alhambra (Spain) - A magnificent fortress and palace complex in Granada, blending Islamic and Christian architectural styles.
  • Masada (Israel) - An ancient fortification on a hilltop, famous for its dramatic history and breathtaking desert views.
